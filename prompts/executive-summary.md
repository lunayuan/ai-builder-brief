# Executive Summary

The exec summary is the "if you read nothing else" section. Write it AFTER all category sections are complete.

## Rules

- **3-5 bullet points.** No more. No less than 3 unless it's genuinely a dead week.
- **Lead with the single most important item.** The one that most directly affects what the reader is building or tracking (informed by `config.identity.why`).
- **Each bullet is ONE sentence.** Not a paragraph. Not two sentences. One.
- **Prioritize items with direct personal implications** over general AI noise.
- **Bold the key entity or event** in each bullet.
- **No filler.** No "This week saw several developments in..." — just the signals.

## Example

```
- **Anthropic launches Claude 4 with 1M-token context** — largest usable context window yet, reshaping what's possible for AI-native products
- **Cursor raises $400M at $9B valuation** — AI coding tools market is consolidating fast, and the winners are pulling away
- **EU AI Act enforcement begins for high-risk systems** — any builder deploying AI in Europe now faces mandatory compliance requirements
```

## "What to Watch Next Week"

After the main category sections (not inside the exec summary), include 2-4 forward-looking items:
- Upcoming regulation deadlines
- Anticipated model releases or product launches
- AI conferences, demo days, or events
- Earnings calls from major AI companies
- Policy decisions affecting AI builders

Format as a simple bullet list under the heading "WHAT TO WATCH NEXT WEEK."
